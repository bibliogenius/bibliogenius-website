The Memory game uses your own book covers to create a matching pairs game. Flip the cards and find all pairs as quickly as possible.

![Memory game view](images/memory-game-view.png)

## 5 difficulty levels

| Level | Grid | Pairs |
|-------|------|-------|
| Easy | 3x2 | 3 |
| Medium | 4x3 | 6 |
| Hard | 4x4 | 8 |
| Expert | 5x4 | 10 |
| Master | 6x5 | 15 |

## How to play

1. Choose a difficulty level
2. Cards are shuffled and placed face down
3. Flip two cards in a row
4. If they match, they stay visible
5. Otherwise, they flip back after a short delay
6. Find all pairs to complete the game

## 3D animation

Each card flips with a smooth 3D animation, making the game experience more immersive.

## Leaderboard

Your best scores are recorded for each level. Compare yourself with your connected contacts and try to beat their records.
