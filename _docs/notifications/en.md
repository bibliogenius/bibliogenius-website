The Notifications screen centralizes all events related to your network: connection requests, loans, book returns, and wishlist matches.

## Notification types

- **Connection request**: a reader wants to connect to your library
- **Connection accepted**: your connection request was accepted
- **Borrow request**: someone wants to borrow one of your books
- **Borrow accepted**: your borrow request was accepted
- **Book returned**: a borrower has returned your book
- **Wishlist match**: a book from your wishlist was found in a contact's library

## Filtering notifications

At the top of the screen, filter chips let you display only certain categories:

- **All**: all notifications
- **Connections**: connection requests and acceptances
- **Loans**: borrow requests, acceptances, and returns
- **Discoveries**: wishlist matches

## Actions

- **Tap a notification**: opens the relevant screen (network, requests, contact's library)
- **Swipe left**: dismisses the notification
- **Mark all as read**: via the button in the action bar
- **Clear all**: via the button in the action bar (with confirmation)
