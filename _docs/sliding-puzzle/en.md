The Sliding Puzzle uses one of your book covers as the image to reconstruct. Slide the tiles into the correct position to reveal the full cover.

## 3 difficulty levels

| Level | Grid | Tiles |
|-------|------|-------|
| Easy | 3x3 | 8 |
| Medium | 4x4 | 15 |
| Hard | 5x5 | 24 |

## How to play

1. Choose a difficulty level
2. A book cover from your library is selected at random
3. The image is split into tiles with one empty space
4. Tap a tile adjacent to the empty space to slide it
5. Arrange all tiles in the correct order to complete the puzzle
6. A reference image is shown to help you

## Scoring

Your score is based on the number of moves and the time taken. Fewer moves and faster completion give a higher score.

## Leaderboard

Your best scores are recorded for each level. Try to beat your own records!
