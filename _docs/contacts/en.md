Go to "Network" to add friends or libraries. You can add borrowers (who can borrow your books) or libraries (whose catalog you can browse).

![Contacts list](images/contacts-list.png)

## Contact types

- **Borrowers**: people you lend books to
- **Libraries**: catalogs you can browse and request loans from

## Add a contact

You can add a contact by scanning their QR code or entering their information manually.
