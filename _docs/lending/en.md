Find the book in your library, tap "Manage Copies", then "Lend". Select a contact to lend to.

![Book details with Lend button](images/book-details.png)

## Steps to lend

1. Go to your library and open the book's detail page
2. Tap "Manage Copies"
3. Select "Lend"
4. Choose which contact to lend to
5. Confirm the loan

## Tracking loans

Your active loans are visible from the "Requests" section. You can mark a book as returned at any time.

![Loans and borrowing view](images/loans-view.png)
