The Audio module automatically searches for free audio versions of your books on open online libraries: LibriVox, Litteratureaudio.com and Internet Archive. Enable the module in your settings, then listen directly from the app.

## Enable the Audio module

1. Go to your Profile
2. Open settings
3. Enable the "Audio" module

![Module settings](images/settings-modules.png)

## Available sources

- **LibriVox**: the largest free audiobook catalog, available in multiple languages
- **Litteratureaudio.com**: high-quality French classics
- **Internet Archive**: very large catalog, varied quality

## Listen to an audiobook

When a free audiobook is available for a book in your library, BiblioGenius detects it automatically. You can then listen directly from the book details page, chapter by chapter.
