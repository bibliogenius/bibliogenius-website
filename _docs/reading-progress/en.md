Open a book and tap "Mark as Reading" to start tracking. Update your progress as you read. When finished, mark it as "Read" to earn XP and unlock achievements!

![Book details with reading status](images/book-details.png)

## Reading statuses

Each book can have a status:

- **To Read**: in your reading queue
- **Reading**: you are currently reading it
- **Read**: finished!
- **Abandoned**: you stopped reading

## Page progress

When a book is "Reading", you can enter which page you're on. The progress bar updates automatically.

## Rewards

Finishing a book earns you XP and can unlock achievements in the gamification system.
