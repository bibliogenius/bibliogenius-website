Privacy Policy for BiblioGenius

Last updated: January 2026

BiblioGenius ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Camera Permission

Our application requires access to your device's camera to scan ISBN barcodes on books. This processing happens locally on your device or is sent to our server solely to identify the book. We do not store or share photos taken with the camera.

Data Collection

We collect basic information about your book library to provide the service. This includes book titles, authors, and ISBNs.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
