To decide what comes next for BiblioGenius, I'd love your opinion: **what matters most to you in a personal library app?**

I've put together a short form to put your priorities at the heart of the next decisions. Should I focus on sharing between friends? Scanning and automatic metadata enrichment? Audiobooks? Gamification? You get to say.

## What to expect

- Takes just **2 minutes** to complete
- **Anonymous**: no personal data requested
- **No account**: no sign-up, no e-mail
- A few simple questions about what matters to you, plus an open space for your ideas

## Take the survey

👉 [Answer the survey](https://framaforms.org/move-bibliogenius-forward-1781766356)

Every answer counts. Whether you already use the app every day or have barely opened it, your perspective helps steer what gets built next.

Thank you for taking these two minutes: it's largely thanks to your feedback that BiblioGenius keeps moving in the right direction.
