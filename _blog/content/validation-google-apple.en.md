Two great pieces of news in one shot: BiblioGenius has just been approved by both Google and Apple (ready to publish). We're now entering the final stretch before the public release on both platforms.

## On Android

The app can now be installed by anyone in France, with no prior enrollment in the testing programme. If you haven't updated yet, do it now:

<https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bibliogenius.app>

## On iPhone

While we wait for the App Store to go public, you still need to go through the TestFlight companion app to access BiblioGenius:

<https://testflight.apple.com/v1/app/6757465461>

## Share freely

If you know people who might enjoy the app, you can now send them these links directly, with no need to enrol them in a testing programme.

Until the public release, feel free to send any improvement ideas or bugs you come across, even the smallest ones, to [contact@bibliogenius.org](mailto:contact@bibliogenius.org). Every piece of feedback helps wrap up this final stage.

Thanks to each and every one of you for being part of the BiblioGenius journey. See you very soon!
