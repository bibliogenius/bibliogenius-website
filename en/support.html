Frequently asked questions

Is BiblioGenius free?

Yes. The app is completely free and open source, released under the AGPL-3.0 license.

Where is my data stored?

Your data stays stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to a server without an action from you. See the Data privacy guide.

Which platforms does BiblioGenius support?

BiblioGenius is available on iOS, Android, macOS, Windows and Linux.

How do I connect to another reader's library?

You can connect via a QR code or an invitation link. See the Connect with friends guide.
