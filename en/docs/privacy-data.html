Where is my data stored

Your library data is stored locally on your device. When you connect with friends, only the books you choose to share are visible to them. You can export a backup anytime from your Profile.

Local storage

All your data (books, shelves, loans, contacts) is stored in a local database on your device. Nothing is sent to a remote server.

Controlled sharing

When you connect with other libraries, you maintain full control over what is shared. Only the books you choose to make visible are visible.

Backups

Export a backup of all your data at any time from the Profile section. You can also re-import them when switching devices.

Encryption

Communications between libraries are end-to-end encrypted (E2EE). Your data stays private.
