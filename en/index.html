Features

Local-First

Your data belongs to you. Works offline, no cloud required.

Network Sharing

Lend your books to trusted friends or neighbors. Create a local and decentralized reading network.

Encrypted & Private

No tracking, no ads. Your data is end-to-end encrypted: no one can read it, not even the server.

Modular

An app that adapts to your needs: reader, family, association, librarian, bookseller, or teacher.
